**Code of Conduct**
We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community.

**Expectations**
- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent; ask for clarification.
- Use inclusive language; no personal attacks.

**Unacceptable behavior**
- Harassment, discrimination, hate speech
- Doxxing, stalking, sustained disruption
- Sexualized language or imagery

**Reporting**
Report to support@budventure.technology with what/where/when and context.
Reports are confidential to maintainers.

**Enforcement**
- Warning → temporary restrictions → ban for severe/repeated issues
- Maintainers’ decisions are final, and this policy may be updated.
